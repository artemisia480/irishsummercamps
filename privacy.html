Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 April 2026

What we collect

When you submit a camp, we may collect your name, email address, and the camp information you provide.

How we use information

We use submitted information to review, verify, and publish camp listings, and to contact you about your submission when needed.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only publish listing information that is relevant to the camp directory.

Website analytics

We use Plausible Analytics to understand overall website traffic and popular pages. This provides aggregated usage metrics (for example page views, referrers, and country-level traffic) and is not used to identify individual visitors.

Retention

Submission records may be retained for moderation, audit, and legal compliance purposes.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by emailing admin@irishsummercamps.ie.
