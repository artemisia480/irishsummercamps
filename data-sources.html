Data Sources & Accuracy

Last updated: 30 April 2026

Source types

  • School or venue pages
  • User-submitted camp details
  • Curated research from publicly available pages

Verification approach

We aim to review data before publishing. However, dates, prices, and availability may change quickly.

Scraping and compliance

Where automated collection is used, we aim to respect website access rules (including robots.txt) and source terms.

Please verify before booking

Always check the organiser website and contact details before making payment or travel decisions.
