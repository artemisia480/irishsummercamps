Corrections & Takedowns

If any listing is incorrect, outdated, or should be removed, email us at hello@irelandsummercamps.ie.

What to include

  • Camp name and URL
  • What needs changing or removing
  • Evidence or official source link
  • Your role (organiser, parent, venue, etc.)

Response target

We aim to review correction and takedown requests within 2 business days.
